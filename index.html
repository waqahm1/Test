Meet Our Team

Bilal Farooq - Standing for Chairman

Bilal Farooq has had the privilege of serving as Chairman for the past three years, working collaboratively to achieve significant milestones. These include organising Eid in the Park with Shah Jalal Mosque, hosting Taste Ramadan and the Interfaith Walk, running sports clubs for our young people, and representing the Gravesham community at events such as the Million March for Palestine.

An active member of the Gravesend and Dartford Muslim Association since 2012, Bilal has consistently worked to foster unity and inclusivity within the community. Under his leadership, initiatives have strengthened interfaith dialogue, supported local causes, and engaged members of all ages.

If re-elected, Bilal aims to further involve youth through workshops, fresh initiatives, and sub-committees, while continuing to prioritise interfaith collaboration and community development. His focus remains on working together to build a stronger, more inclusive future for all.

Imran Tahir - Standing for Secretary

Imran has been a member of the committee for 3 years and has made significant contributions. This year alone, he has claimed gift aid on behalf of the mosque, been part of the education committee and arranged security guards for the mosque.

Professionally, Imran is an economist at a leading research institute. He holds degrees from the LSE and UCL and is currently completing his PhD in economics. Imran has presented his research to the government and civil service and has been featured on the BBC and in the Financial Times.

More important than his CV, Imran is driven by a sincere desire to work tirelessly to advance our community.

Adnan Khan - Standing for Treasurer

Adnan Khan is a qualified chartered accountant with over 30 years of experience, which makes him perfect for the role of treasurer.

He performed Itikaf at our mosque in 2022 and ever since has been closely associated with the mosque ever since.

Adnan is an active volunteer with a charity called Future Frontiers, which provides mentoring to young people from disadvantaged backgrounds.

He plans to bring career coaching programmes to the local community. Adnan is committed to using his extensive experience in governance and leadership, gained in five countries, to serve the Muslim community in Gravesend.
